Assetto Corsa Setup Diff Tool

Built to quickly compare two setups. This is a work in progress as you can see. If you upload setups from two different cars with different parameters it may not work properly.

Setups are not uploaded anywhere. All evaluations and comparisons are done in your browser. You can inspect the network activity and verify.
